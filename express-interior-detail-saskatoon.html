Express Interior Detail

A quick clean for your vehicle

Just looking for quick refresh of your daily driver?

Our Express Detail service is our entry level service that will leave your interior clean, without spending too much time or money.

We will do a full wipedown of your interior to remove surface dirt and debris on all surfaces. As well as a thorough vacuum of the entire interior and trunk.

This service is ideal if you have had your vehicle detailed recently, or are just not looking to get a perfectly clean interior this time.

This is not a deep clean service. If you are looking to have pet hair, muddy carpets, or stains removed then our Full Interior Detail service wold be a better option for you.

Express Detail Process

1
Remove Loose Items

We will remove obvious garbage and collect loose items. Loose items will be organized and left in the vehicle for you.

2
Wipe down of vinyl and plastics

We will clean the door panels, dashboard, and center console of your vehicle.

3
Thorough vacuum

A detailed vacuum of your vehicles seats, carpet, and trunk area.

4
Clean interior glass

Interior glass will be cleaned and wiped to a streak free shine.

Express Interior Detail FAQs

Do you come to me?

Yes, we are a fully-contained mobile detailing unit. We can perform the service at your home, apartment, or work location. We service Saskatoon, Warman, Martensville, Dundurn, and surrounding areas.

Will you remove certain stains?

Yes if you have specific areas you would like shampooed or extra detail taken into consideration please let us know.

How long does the detail take?

The duration widely varies by the condition of the vehicle. Generally the interior express detail can take anywhere from 1 to 2 hours for any size vehicle.
