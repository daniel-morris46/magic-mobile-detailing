Ceramic Coating

The ultimate service for your vehicle's exterior

Years of shine and protection

Your vehicle is more than just a means of transportation - it's an investment. This high quality ceramic coating service will protect and enhance the appearance of your vehicle for years to come.

Ceramic coating is a cutting-edge solution that provides an extra layer of protection for your vehicle's paint. Unlike traditional waxes or sealants, ceramic coating forms a permanent bond with your vehicle's paint, creating a hydrophobic barrier that repels water, dirt, and other contaminants.

The benefits of ceramic coating include:

  • Long-lasting protection: Ceramic coating can last up to five years, protecting your vehicle from scratches, UV rays, and oxidation.
  • Enhanced appearance: Ceramic coating adds depth and shine to your vehicle's paint, making it look better than ever before.
  • Easy maintenance: Ceramic coating makes it easier to clean your vehicle, saving you time and effort in the long run.

Yes, we're a fully-contained mobile detailing unit. We can perform the wash at your home, apartment, or work location. We service Saskatoon, Warman, Martensville, Dundurn, and surrounding areas.

How long will the ceramic coating last?

The longevity of your ceramic coating will depend on many factors such as how often you drive and wash your vehicle. CarPro states that their real-world durability claim for CQ.UK 3.0 is 18-24 months.

How can I help maintain my ceramic coating?

Your can help maintain your vehicle's ceramic coating by washing your vehicle regularly, avoiding harsh chemicals from automatic car washes, and parking your vehicle inside.
