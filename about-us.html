About Us

Meet your detailer!

Magic Mobile Detailing is owned and operated by Daniel Morris. With the goal to create the most convenient auto detailing service in Saskatoon Dan created Magic Mobile Detailing in 2022 to provide residents of Saskatoon and surrounding areas with a revolutionary new way to maintain the appearance of their vehicle.

Prior to detailing Dan worked as a software developer. With his Computer Science Degree from the U of S he worked as a software developer for 4 years before leaving his employment to pursue this endeavour.

Dan has always enjoyed working on and maintaining his vehicles. In an effort to pursue this passion further Dan created Magic Mobile Detailing as a way to share this love for vehicles by offering a service in his community.

Our Setup

In order to best serve you the Magic Mobile Detailing Van is kitted out with all the best equipment and products to give your vehicle the best clean it deserves.

We bring our own water and power as a measure of efficiency and convenience for you. No need to worry where you are located we will be able to service your vehicle on your driveway, appartment, or work location.

Our detailing products vary but are all specifically picked as the highest quality offerings to use for your vehicle. Our favourite brands include P&S, Meguiar's, CarPro, and Chemical Guys.
