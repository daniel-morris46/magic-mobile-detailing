Express Detail

A quick clean for your vehicle

Just looking for a quick refresh of your daily driver?

Our Express Detail service is our entry level service that will leave your vehicle clean, without spending too much time or cost.

Your vehicle's interior will be wiped down and vacuumed and the exterior will be hand washed.

This service is ideal if you have had your vehicle detailed recently, or are not looking for a thorough deep clean.

This is not a deep clean service. If you are looking to have pet hair, muddy carpets, or stains removed then the Full Detail service wold be a better option for you.

Express Detail Process

1
Remove loose items

Garbage will be removed and your loose items will be organized and left in the vehicle for you.

2
Wipe down of vinyl and plastics

Your door panels, dashboard, and center console of your vehicle will be cleaned.

3
Thorough Vacuum

A detailed vacuum of your vehicle's seats, carpet, and trunk area.

4
Snow Foam Pressure Wash

Your vehicle's exterior will be foamed and pressure washed to remove loose dirt.

5
Hand Wash & Towel Dry

Your vehicle will be hand washed removing dirt, bugs, and road grime.

6
Glass Cleaning

All glass will be cleaned and wiped to a streak free shine.

Express Detail FAQs

Do you come to me?

Yes, we are a fully-contained mobile detailing unit. We can perform the service at your home, apartment, or work location. We service Saskatoon, Warman, Martensville, Dundurn, and surrounding areas.

How do you take payment?

We take all forms of payment including credit, debit, cash, cheque or e-transfer.

How long does this service take?

The duration varies by the size and condition of the vehicle. Generally the express detail can take anywhere from 2 to 3 hours for any size vehicle.

Will you remove certain stains?

Yes, if you have specific areas you would like shampooed or extra detail taken into consideration please let us know. There may be an additional fee for stain removal.
