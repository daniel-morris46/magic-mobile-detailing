Jade Unterschute

2016 - 2024

TD Futsal has been a huge part of my life for 8 years. Looking back, It's amazing how the game has shaped me as both a soccer player and a futsal player. Futsal has a way of teaching us important skills like quick thinking, agility, and teamwork. Which I now use in my everyday life apart from just sports.

Through my 8 years I’ve made countless memories and friendships along the way. I’ve faced both success and failure, but through all the ups and downs TD Futsal has made me a better person and player. Without TD I wouldn’t be the person of player I am today, and I’m so thankful I got to be around both the players and the amazing coaches. TD Futsal will always be my Family and Home. Thank you for a great 8 years.
