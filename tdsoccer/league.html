Great Plains Futsal League

The Great Plains Futsal League started in November 2023. This league is the product of 5 clubs joining forces to grow the game of Futsal in Saskatchewan: Yorkton United, Swift Current United, Excelsior FC (Moose Jaw), Queen City United Soccer Club (Regina) and TD Soccer Futsal Academy. Each club hosted a weekend of league games, and the final weekend of playoffs and finals was played in Saskatoon the weekend of February 9-11, 2024. Our first year of the league was a huge success, with highly competitive matches and nail-biting finals. Next year we will build on this success and add more clubs to the league to make it even more competitive.
