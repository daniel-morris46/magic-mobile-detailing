Programs

All our programs are game based, which means that at least 90% of our training is in a game setting and up to 10% is technical/tactical instruction. Our objective is to use the game to teach fundamentals of futsal. In the U10-U12 groups, we emphasize individual technical skills. In the U14-U18 groups we introduce and develop futsal tactical skills.

Skill Development (U10-U12 and U14-U18 that are looking for additional training)

The objective of this program is to work on technical skills and is geared towards players who are looking for additional training without committing to the competitive stream. Training will be once a week and it starts mid-October to December and January to March. There will be opportunities for exhibition games during the program.

Competitive (U14-U18

The objective of this program is to provide players with the opportunity to play futsal, not just as additional training, but at a more competitive level. Training for this group is twice a week, and players have the opportunity to participate in the futsal league, tournaments and exhibition games.
