Strategic Plan

TDsoccer is pleased to announce that it has completed an intensive planning process resulting in the creation of a strategic plan that will guide our organization in the delivery of futsal based programming.

We have developed a vision, a mission statement, and identified a core set of values that will guide all aspects of our decision making and programs:

1. Vision:

Every child has the opportunity to develop and grow through futsal (indoor soccer), regardless of their circumstances.

2. Mission:

We are committed to providing affordable development specific futsal training and ball mastery to dedicated youth players in a fun and respectful environment.

3. Values:

Dedication, hard work, team work, community, inclusivity, and affordability.

4. Strategic Plan:

The areas at the core of the Strategic Plan are player development, coaching, community, partnerships, organizational structure, and affordability.

TDsoccer would like to thank the SSA for offering this initiative and Paul Varian of Capitis Consulting for sharing his expertise and assistance.

We are very grateful to our partners in the soccer community who met with us and provided their time and input for the benefit of our programs and out of a mutually desirable objective to enhance the soccer experience for our athletes and the benefit of the game.
