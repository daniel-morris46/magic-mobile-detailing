Who are we – what do we do – why should you care

The TDsoccer Futsal Academy is Saskatoons only Futsal specific academy sanctioned and supported by the Saskatchewan Soccer Association. We provide futsal specific training to youth players. Our program has a proven track record of developing a players technical ability to a high level. Futsal is one of the fastest growing indoor sports with many Universities offering programming and scholorships.

Overview

The U15 Boys program aim is to play the game of futsal with a focus on ball possesion and good understading of defensive formation as a team. Player rotation is also taught at this age level as well as set plays, transition, and game management.

Each training session will typically begin with a shooting drill with conditions added to achieve learning opportunities and get players ready for a game. The philosophy of TDsoccer is very much to let the game be the teacher so we like to get playing games as soon as we can.

Game Format

5v5 – including goalkeeper

Court size – typically 30 meters x 20 meters

Goal size – 3 meters wide x 2 meters high (standard futsal size goal)

Game duration – Two 20-minute halves with a 5-minute half time break

Game days at Aden Bowman Collegiate

These game days will be delivered in a jamboree format where we will invite guest teams from outside of our academy to come and play games. Four teams will be made, and each team will play each other in a round robin format. Scores and standings will be recorded for this age level. Games will be refereed by TDsoccer coaches with assistant coaches (parent volunteers) running the benches for each team.

Game duration for jamboree games – Two 15-minute halves with a 10-minute half time break.
