Wash and Wax

Give your ride a stunning shine

A simple service to give your vehicle a glossy look with a layer of protection

When was the last time you waxed your vehicle?

This service will leave your vehicle will leave it with a slick glossy look that is exceptionally smooth to the touch. When we are done with your vehicle it will feel as smooth as glass.

Waxing your vehile is also important as it adds a layer of protection to your vehicles paint. Your paintwork will have a layer of protection against road grime, and swirl marks from contact car washes. This will extend the life of your vehicles paint and will help prevent neccecary paint correction down the line.

For a complete full detail service this service is great when paired with our Engine Cleaning and/or Full Interior Detail services.

The Magic Wash and Wax Process

1

Give your vehicle our Premium Hand Wash

2

Use an iron decontamination spray to remove brake dust and iron deposits from your vehicles paintwork

3

Clay bar the vehicles paint work to remove remaining imperfections and embedded road grit

4

Towel dry with a soft microfiber drying towel

5

Apply a high quality wax or sealant by hand or machine to the vehicles paintwork

6

Buff to a stunning shine
