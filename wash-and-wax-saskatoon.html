Wash and Wax

Give your ride a stunning shine

A simple service to give your vehicle a glossy look with a layer of protection

When was the last time you waxed your vehicle?

This service will leave your vehicle with a slick glossy look that is exceptionally smooth to the touch. When we are done with your vehicle it will feel as smooth as glass.

Waxing your vehicle is also important as it adds a layer of protection to your vehicle's paint. Your paintwork will have a layer of protection against road grime and swirl marks from contact car washes. This will extend the life of your vehicle's paint and will help prevent neccecary paint correction down the line.

To get your vehicle in top condition it is recommended to pair this service with our Full Interior Detail and/or Engine Cleaning services.

The Magic Wash and Wax Process

1

Give your vehicle our Premium Hand Wash.

2

Use an iron decontamination spray to remove brake dust and iron deposits from your vehicle's paintwork.

3

Clay bar the vehicle's paint work to remove remaining imperfections and embedded road grit.

4

Towel dry with a soft microfiber drying towel.

5

Apply a high quality wax or sealant by hand or machine to the vehicle's paintwork.

6

Buff to a stunning shine.
