When was the last time you waxed your vehicle?

This service will leave your vehicle with a slick glossy look that is exceptionally smooth to the touch. After this service your vehicle it will feel as smooth as glass.

Waxing your vehicle is important as it adds a layer of protection to your vehicle's paint. Your paintwork will have a layer of protection against road grime and swirl marks from contact car washes. This will extend the life of your vehicle's paint and will help prevent neccecary paint correction down the line.

This service is offered as an add on to the Full Detail and/or Engine Cleaning services.

The Magic Wash and Wax Process

1

Give your vehicle our Premium Hand Wash.

2

Use an iron decontamination spray to remove brake dust and iron deposits from your vehicle's paintwork.

3

Clay bar the vehicle's paint work to remove remaining imperfections and embedded road grit.

4

Towel dry with a soft microfiber drying towel.

5

Apply a high quality wax or sealant by hand or machine to the vehicle's paintwork.

6

Buff to a stunning shine.

How long does a car wax last?

On average a car wax should last 3 months however this can change based on weather conditions, dirt build-up, and other factors.

Can I wash my vehicle after a wax?

Yes, although the more often you wash your vehicle the quicker the wax will fade.
