Headlight Restoration

Restore your headlights to improve clarity and nighttime visibility

Your vehicle will look years newer with clear shiny headlights

Have your headlights developed a foggy hazy look to them? Or have you noticed your visibility at night is not what it once was?

Our headlight resotation process will remove the outer oxidized layer of your headlights that is causing these issues.

During the process we will wet sand the outer layer of your headlights away then polish and protect the headlights to prevent the issue from reocurring.

This service is offered as an add on service to one of our exterior wash packages. Check out our Premium Hand Wash or Wash and Wax services to learn more about those.

Our Headlight Restoration Process

1
Inspect headlights

We will inspect your headlights to determine how aggressive to begin with the restoration.

2
Mask Off the Headlight

We will use automotive masking tape around your headlight to ensure we do not alter the surrounding bodywork.

3
Wet Sanding

We will use increasing grit sandpaper with soapy water to remove the outer oxidized layer of your headlight surface.

4
Cut and Polish

We will compound and polish the headlights to bring them to a haze free shine.

5
Apply Coating

We will apply a specially designed coating to the headlights to ensure they do not degrade in appearance.

Headlight Restoration FAQs

Do you come to me?

Yes, we're a fully-contained mobile detailing unit. We can perform the wash at your home, apartment, or work location. We service Saskatoon, Warman, Martensville, Dundurn, and surrounding areas.

How long does this service take?

Depending on the size and condition of the headlights this service can take between 45 to 90 minutes.

My headlights are really bad, could you still restore them?

With any headlights we will be able to make them look much better with this service. However if your headlights have cracks, rock chips, or the damage to the plastic is beneath the surface of the headlights we may not be able to get them in absolutely perfect condition.

How can I make sure my headlights do not get like this again

The last step of our process is a protective coating of the headlights. This coating is specially made for vehicle headlights and will protect your headlights from clouding over again.
