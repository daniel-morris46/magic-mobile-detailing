Our Engine Cleaning Process

1
Inspect Engine Bay

We will inspect your engine bay and let you know if you have any leaks or neccecary wear items to take note of for your next vehicles service.

2
Cover sensative areas

We will cover your vehicels sensitive electronics and air intake.

3
Deep cleaning

Your engine will get a thorough clean using degreasing chemical and brush agitation to remove as much dirt and dust as possible.

4
Dry and blow out standing water

Using drying towels and compressed air we will remove any remaining water from your clean engine bay.

5
Dress plastics

Your engine cover and plastics will recieve a layer of plastic protection to bring a shine to your clean engine.
