Our Engine Cleaning Process

1
Inspect Engine Bay

We will inspect your engine bay and let you know if you have any leaks or necessary wear items to take note of for your vehicle's next service.

2
Cover sensative areas

We will cover your vehicle's sensitive electronics and air intake.

3
Deep cleaning

Your engine will get a thorough clean using degreasing chemicals and brush agitation to remove as much dirt and dust as possible.

4
Dry and blow out standing water

Using drying towels and compressed air we will remove any remaining water from your clean engine bay.

5
Dress plastics

Your engine cover and plastics will recieve a layer of plastic protection to bring a shine to your clean engine.

Engine Cleaning FAQs

Do you come to me?

Yes, we're a fully-contained mobile detailing unit. We can perform the wash at your home, apartment, or work location. We service Saskatoon, Warman, Martensville, Dundurn, and surrounding areas.

How do you take payment?

We take all forms of payment including credit, debit, cash, cheque or e-transfer.

Do you use a pressure washer on the engine?

Yes. We use our pressure washer with a different nozzle that does not spray water at high pressure. We use this nozzle for the engine bay as to not damage fragile connections and components of your engine with high pressure water.

Will this cause damage to my engine?

No it will not. With our process of covering sensative electronics, low pressure water, and blowing out and drying the engine once we are done we will be sure no damage is caused.
