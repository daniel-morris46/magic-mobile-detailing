Full Interior Detail FAQs

Do you come to me?

Yes, we are a fully-contained mobile detailing unit. We can perform the service at your home, apartment, or work location. We service Saskatoon, Warman, Martensville, Dundurn, and surrounding areas.

Will you shampoo my carpets?

That depends. If the carpets are muddy or contain stains we will certainly shampoo the carpet. We can always shampoo all cloth surfaces if you would like. How much shampooing we do is considered when quoting the service during initial inspection.

How long does the detail take?

The duration widely varies by the condition of the vehicle. Generally the interior detail can take anywhere from 1 to 4 hours for any size vehicle.

Will my vehicle be damp when you are done?

If we do shampoo your carpets then yes your vehicle will be slightly damp. We will do our best to extract and wipe away as much moisture as we can but some dampness is inevitable.

Do you remove seats?

No we do not do seat removal. Our tools and techniques will do a very good job getting around the base of your seats so that they should not need to be removed.

Will you be able to remove this stain?

Hopefully! It is always hard to tell if a stain will come out without trying first. What we do ensure is that we do everything we can with our variety of chemicals and equipment to remove as many stains as possible.
