The Magic Wash Process

1

Clean rims and tires.

2

Rinse vehicle to remove top layer of dirt and salt stuck to the vehicle

3

Snow foam the vehicle and allow dwell time for soap to work on the surface to help remove stuck dirt

4

Hand wash the vehicle using a microfiber wash mitt and the two bucket method

5

Rinse the vehicle to remove all foam and wiped away contaminents

6

Apply a spray wax or sealent for extra shine and protection (lasts a few weeks)

7

Towel dry with a soft microfiver drying towel

8

Clean exterior glass and side mirrors

9

Dress tires to make them shine
