The Full Detail Service is a top to bottom deep cleaning service of your vehicle.

This comprehensive detailing service is a meticulous cleaning and conditioning of all areas of your vehicle's interior and exterior.

This service is ideal for those who want to give their vehicle a new lease on life or get it ready for sale.

If it can be cleaned we will clean it. We can not guarantee that every last stain and blemish will be removed however we use all the best products and equipment to ensure we do the best work possible.

1

3

10
Glass Cleaning

All glass will be cleaned and wiped to a streak free shine.

Full Detail FAQs

Do you come to me?

Yes, we are a fully-contained mobile detailing unit. We can perform the service at your home, apartment, or work location. We service Saskatoon, Warman, Martensville, Dundurn, and surrounding areas.

How do you take payment?

We take all forms of payment including credit, debit, cash, cheque or e-transfer.

How long does the detail take?

The duration widely varies by the condition of the vehicle. Generally the full detail can take anywhere from 3 to 5 hours for any size vehicle.

Will you shampoo my fabrics?

That depends. If your cloth seats or carpets are muddy or contain stains we will certainly shampoo them. We can always shampoo all cloth surfaces if you would like. How much shampooing we do is considered when quoting the service during initial inspection.

Will my vehicle be damp when you are done?

If we do shampoo your carpets, then yes your vehicle will be slightly damp. We will do our best to extract and wipe away as much moisture as we can, but some dampness is inevitable.

Will you be able to remove this stain?

Hopefully! It is always hard to tell if a stain will come out without trying first. What we do ensure is that we will do everything we can with our variety of chemicals and equipment to remove as many stains as possible.

Do you remove seats?

No, we do not do seat removal. Our tools and techniques do a good job getting under your seats so that they do not need to be removed.

How often should I have my vehicle detailed?

We suggest you have your vehicle detailed twice a year, however the duration can change based on your vehicle's condition. The more often you detail your vehicle the easier it is to maintain.
